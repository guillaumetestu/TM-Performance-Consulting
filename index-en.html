Our approach

Field-based.
Not theory.

Through our expertise and hands-on experience, we equip our clients with all the tools needed to succeed in professional football. We provide expert advice to inform their strategic decisions and guide them through the planning and implementation of tailored recommendations.

We do not offer off-the-shelf models. Every mission starts with a deep understanding of your reality — your ambitions, your constraints, your competitive schedule — before we define together the most appropriate roadmap.

01

Strategic framing

Interviews with the board and owners to clarify objectives, constraints and true ambitions.

02

Operational immersion

On-site presence, training observations, interviews with each department. 5 to 15 days depending on the structure.

03

Analysis & benchmarking

Identification of priority levers, comparison with best practices at the target level.

04

Action plan & follow-up

Co-construction of a prioritised roadmap, long-term accompaniment and regular adjustments.
